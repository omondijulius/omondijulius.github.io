A VIDEOGRAPHER'S STORY

Life as a videographer and cameraman in the ghetto is not easy but truth be told their is alot of untold stories in Kibra , i met new and exciting people everyday doing their hustles passionetly including other filmakers who are eager to tell their stories to the world.

Also being a really big supporter of the SDGs Sustainable Development Goals i support SDG 6 which is clean water and sanitation, all this goals should be known and understood by everyone for the betterment of our planet.
